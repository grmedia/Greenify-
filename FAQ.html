Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about Greenify, your data, and how to make the most of our sustainability tools.

General

What is Greenify and how does it work?

Greenify is a web platform that helps you track and reduce your environmental footprint. Log your daily activities like transport and energy use, and we calculate your estimated carbon emissions using scientific emission factors, then provide personalized tips and challenges to live greener.

Is Greenify free to use?

Yes, Greenify offers a generous free tier with all core features. Optional premium features (advanced analytics, exclusive challenges) are available via subscription to support our mission.

How does Greenify calculate my carbon footprint?

We combine peer‑reviewed emission factors, your activity data, and AI to estimate your carbon footprint. Our models follow standards from the IPCC and governmental environmental agencies, and they are regularly updated for accuracy.

Data & Privacy

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. We use end‑to‑end encryption for all personal data, never share identifiable information without your consent, and comply with GDPR and CCPA. You can manage your data in Settings.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export your activity history and carbon footprint data as a CSV file from Settings > Data & Privacy.

Features & Usage

How do I connect smart home devices?

Go to Settings > Smart Home Integration and follow the prompts to securely link compatible devices like smart thermostats or energy monitors.

What are Eco‑Challenges?

Eco‑Challenges are fun, goal‑based activities that help you adopt sustainable habits. Browse and join them from the Challenges section; some are personalized to your lifestyle.

Can I chat with an AI for eco‑tips?

Yes! Our AI Eco‑Assistant is available 24/7 via the Chatbot page. It answers sustainability questions and gives instant, personalized advice.

Support

How can I contact support?

Email us at support@greenify.com or use the "Report a Bug" option in Settings. We typically respond within 24 hours.
